About The Company


BOOKMARKED is an online bookshop made by four Philippine Science High School Students for an enjoyable browse through some recommended reads. It offers a cozy theme and affordable prices, as well as fast delivery and for a cause! For every book you buy, the profit would be donated to organizations that teach and take care of leff-fortunate children. For an enjoyable and purposeful shopping experience, BOOKMARKED is the place to buy!
